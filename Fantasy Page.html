Fantasy Sub-Genres

High Fantasy

Many Fantasy books fall under the sub-genre of high fantasy. In these books, the world is completely different than ours, and full of imaginary creatures, objects, people and more. One very famous high fantasy book is The Lord of the Rings by JRR Tolkien.

Low Fantasy

Low fantasy books are set in a wold very similar to ours. In this world, magical things, creatures or animals are either a secret or very rare and isolated from the nonmagical world. One famous low fantasy book that almost everyone must know about is Harry Potter by JK Rowling.

Magical Realism

Magical realism books are also set in a world similar to ours. The only thing different, is that magic or magical creatures are commonplace and well integrated into society. This could mean that chatting with a ghost is something very commonplace to the protagonist. One famous magical realism book is One Hundred Years of Solitude by Gabriel Garcia Marquez.
