Sci-Fi Sub-Genres

Time Travel

Many Science Fiction books fall under the sub-genre of time travel. In these books, a major part of the plot includes traveling through time, one example of a famous time travel book is "The Time Machine" by HG Wells.

Dystopian Sci-Fi

I have read many books that fall under the Science Fiction sub-genre of Dystopia. In Dystopian Science Fiction, the main plot is in a dystopian world. That would mean that there might be a lot of struggle and pain, or that the world has a very barren and desolate landscape. One example of a famous dystopian science fiction book would be "1984" by George Orwell.
