Romance Sub-Genres

Young Adult (YA) Romance

Many Romance books are written for young adults. In these books, the main characters are teenagers, and the book tries to relate to young adults. One pretty famous YA romance book is "The Selection" by Kiera Cass.

LGBTQ+ Romance

LGBTQ+ books are books with romance that is LGBTQ+. That means that it is a lesbian, gay or bisexual romance story. It could also mean that the main characters are trans or queer, or something else. One famous LGBTQ+ book is "Simon vs the Homo Sapiens Agenda" by Becky Albertalli.

Magical Realism

Magical realism books are also set in a world similar to ours. The only thing different, is that magic or magical creatures are commonplace and well integrated into society. This could mean that chatting with a ghost is something very commonplace to the protagonist. One famous magical realism book is One Hundred Years of Solitude by Gabriel Garcia Marquez.
