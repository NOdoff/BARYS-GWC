Sci-fi

Also known as Science fiction, it may or may not be the most

amazing genre ever. It's futuristic, space-ey?, and very cool. Extra

points for books with aliens, spaceships and laser-beams. Different

sub-genres include Time-Travel, Dystopian Sci-Fi, and of course the common

and Mainstream Sci-Fi.

For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Fantasy

Fantasy is a great genre of speculative fiction that takes place in an

imaginary setting. It can include dragons, knights, and castles, but it could

also take place in an abandoned city full of werewolves or ducks. Different

sub-genres include High Fantasy, Low Fantasy, and Magical Realism.

For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Romance

Romance is when characters fall in love. It can happen

at any time, in any genre, and with anyone. Different sub-genres

include Young Adult, LGBTQ+, and Comedy.

For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com
