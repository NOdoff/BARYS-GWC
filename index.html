Welcome to BARYS! This interractive website was made to help all kinds of people learn more about different books and genres they might be interested in through exciting text-based stories and eloquently crafted summaries written by yours truly! At the moment this website has three genres to chose from; Science Fiction, Fantasy, and Romance. To get started, just click on the icon in the side bar that sounds the most interesting (or scroll down using your mouse/clicker) and read the aformentioned summaries about each genre. Once you find one that strikes your fancy just click on the link in the description. This will take you to a page that houses book recommendations, games, and so much more! Now go, young grasshopper, and start your journey to becoming a bonafide bibliophile!

Sci-fi

Also known as Science fiction, it may or may not be the most amazing genre ever. It's futuristic, space-ey?, and very cool. Extra points for books with aliens, spaceships and laser-beams. For the game, click this link: Science Fiction

Fantasy

Fantasy is a great genre of speculative fiction that takes place in an imaginary setting. It can include dragons, knights, and castles, but it could also take place in an abandoned city full of werewolves or ducks. For the game, click this link: Fantasy

Romance

Romance is when characters fall in love. It can happen at any time, in any genre, and with anyone. Many romance novels also fall into different genres, such as historical or paranormal. For the game, click this link: Romance
