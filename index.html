Sci-fi

Also known as Science fiction, it may or may not be the most amazing genre ever.
	It's futuristic, space-ey?, and very cool. Extra points for books with aliens,
	spaceships and laser-beams. For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Fantasy

Fantasy is a great genre of speculative fiction that takes place in an
	imaginary setting. It can include dragons, knights, and castles, but it could
	also take place in an abandoned city full of werewolves or ducks. For the
	game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Romance

Romance is when characters fall in love. It can happen at any time, in any
	genre, and with anyone. Many romance novels also fall into different genres, such
	as historical or paranormal. For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Horror

Horror novels are much like horror movies, as they aim to scare or
	thrill readers. Many horror novels include supernatural creatures, but it can
	often be psychological as well. For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com
