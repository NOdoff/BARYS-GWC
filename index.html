Sci-fi

Also known as Science fiction, it may or may not be the most

amazing genre ever. It's futuristic, space-ey?, and very cool. Extra

points for books with aliens, spaceships and laser-beams. Different

sub-genres include time-travel, dystopian sci-fi, and of course the common

and mainstream sci-fi.

For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Fantasy

Fantasy is a great genre of speculative fiction that takes place

in an imaginary setting. It can include dragons, knights, and castles, but it

could also take place in an abandoned city full of werewolves or ducks.

Different sub-genres include High Fantasy, Low Fantasy, and Magical Realism.

For the game, click this link: thisisalink.com

Romance

Romance is when characters fall in love. It can happen

at any time, in any genre, and with anyone. Many romance novels

also fall into different genres, such as historical or paranormal.
