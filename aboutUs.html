About the Creators

This website was made by five high schoolers who met in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Amazon Labs126. We were brought together by our shared love of reading and our desire to share our passion with others. We made this website over the course of a week and a half, and our website name comes from the first letter of each founder's name.
